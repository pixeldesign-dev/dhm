Three decades of formulating hygiene products for the people who keep wards, kitchens and laundries running.

Est. 1996Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu

Company Introduction

A formulation house, not just a supplier.

Dirt Hunter Molecules was founded in 1996 with a narrow focus: build cleaning and hygiene products that perform consistently in environments where there's no room for error — hospital wards, commercial kitchens and high-turnover laundries.

That focus has shaped everything since. Rather than reselling generic formulations, we develop, test and refine our own product lines across hospital hygiene, laundry care and housekeeping, backed by documentation our customers' compliance teams can rely on.

  • ✓In-house formulation and batch testing
  • ✓MSDS documentation on every product
  • ✓Direct technical support for procurement teams
